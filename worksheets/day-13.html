The Three Levels of Travel Income.

Income Is a Position, Not Just an Outcome.

Income Is a Position

Most travel professionals talk about income as if it happens to them. CEOs talk about income as a position — a function of how the business is built to earn. Two professionals can sell the same trip for the same price to the same client and earn completely different income from it. The difference is their position in the industry's income architecture. Today, you identify where you are now (or where you are starting), where your vision points you, and what the gap or path between those two positions actually is.

§ IYour Current Operating Status

Before you select an income level, identify which path applies to you. This determines which sections of the worksheet you will complete.

§An Inclusion Reminder

Both paths are equally valid. The agents who have been operating at one level for years often wish they had done this work first. If you are starting fresh, you are not behind — you are at the cleanest possible starting point.
